RSVP: CS Women Panel, November 5th

On November 5th, at 6 PM in Alden Hall, the Department of Computer Science and the departmental Women in CS group will host a Women Alumni Panel. During this video conference panel, successful women computer scientists will give career preparation advice, talk about navigating a career in computing as women, and answer questions from the audience. Following the panel, there will be sign up sheets for individual mentoring with these and other alumni women. Please RSVP if you are able to attend.
